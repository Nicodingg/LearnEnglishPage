Welcome to grammar!

What do you want to learn today?



Nouns: Includes gender, number (singular/plural), and cases (nominative, genitive, etc.)


Pronouns: Personal pronouns, possessive pronouns, reflexive pronouns, demonstrative pronouns, etc.


Adjectives: Description of nouns, comparatives, and superlatives.


Verbs: Verb tenses (present, past, future), aspects (simple, continuous, perfect), passive voice, modal verbs, etc.


Adverbs: Modifiers of verbs, adjectives, and other adverbs.


Prepositions: Spatial and temporal relationships.


Conjunctions: Words that join clauses, phrases, or words.


Articles: "a", "an", and "the".


Determiners: Words that identify or quantify nouns.


Sentences: Sentence structure, types of sentences (declarative, interrogative, imperative, exclamatory), clauses (independent, subordinate), etc.
